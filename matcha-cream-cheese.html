matcha cream cheese bagel

18 August 2020 / Bread

Bitter creaminess

I absolutely love these bagels. This week was my first time trying to make bagels, and I will definitely be making these regularly. Baking these was so therapeutic, and the aesthetics of these matcha bagels add to the calm vibe! Plus, these bagels are STUFFED with matcha cream cheese!

Ingredients

Bun
* 2 cups flour
* 2 Tbsp sugar
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 instant dry yeast
* 3/4 cup warm water
* 1 Tbsp matcha powder

Filling
* 1 tsp matcha powder
* 4 Tbsp cream cheese
* 1 Tbsp powdered sugar

Boiled water
* 1 pot of water
* 2 Tbsp sugar

1. In a large bowl, mix together dry ingredients: sugar, salt, flour, and yeast

2. Slowly add in warm water and integrate all the ingredients. Add more water if the dough is too dry, and add more flour if the dough is too sticky.

3. Cover dough with a damp, warm towel for 30 minutes

4. Sprinkle in the matcha, cut the dough into 8 pieces, and roll each piece into a ball. Let this rest for 20 minutes

5. Meanwhile, whip the cream cheese until fluffy with a hand mixer. Add in the matcha powder and powdered sugar

6. Roll each ball of dough into a long, flat strip. The dough should be around 2 inches by 8 inches. Along the long end, add in the whipped matcha cream cheese

7. Roll up the matcha cream cheese and form the bagel by bringing the ends together. Let the dough rest for 30 more minuttes. Preheat the oven for 360F

8. Prepare boiled water and sprinkle in sugar. When the bagel is ready, put in the bagel and boil each side for a minute each

9. Take the bagel out of the boiling water and let it rest on a baking pan

10. Bake the bagels for 10 minutes at 360F. Then raise the temperature to 390F

11. Bagels are ready when the top is slightly brown! Enjoy!
